### Project Title
Analyzing U.S. Bikeshare Data

### Description
This project computes statistics derived from bikeshare data for three American cities: Chicago, New York City, and Washington.  The user selects the city and optionally filters the data by month and day of the week.  Statistics computed include:

Most frequent **times** of travel:
- Most common month
- Most common day of the week
- Most common starting hour

Most popular **stations** and **trips**:
- Most popular starting station
- Most popular ending station
- Most popular trip

Trip **durations**:
- Total duration of all trips
- Average trip duration

Information about **users**:
- User types
- Gender
- Birth years (earliest, most recent, most common)
